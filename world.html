Malaysia

Located in South Asia, India is home to over 1.4 billion people, making it the most populous democracy in the entire world. The country is known for its diverse landscape, plethora of cultures, religions, and rich history

"Malaysia has a multicultural heritage, lush rainforests, and beautiful beaches. It has four UNESCO-designated World Heritage Sites. These include the Lenggong Valley, an archaeological heritage site worth mentioning, George Town and Melaka's beautiful cities, Kinabalu Park, and Gunung Mulu National Park."
